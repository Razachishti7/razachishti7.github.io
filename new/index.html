Going Public

Going public is the process of offering securities where generally common stock of a privately owned company for sale to the general public. The first time these securities are offered is referred to as an initial public offering or IPO. The benefits of “go public?”

  • To access public capital markets and raise money to expand operations;
  • To acquire other companies with publicly traded stock as the currency;
  • To attract and retain talented employees;
  • To diversify and reduce investor holdings;
  • To provide liquidity for shareholders; and To enhance a company’s reputation.
